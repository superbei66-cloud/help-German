Popular FAQs

Here are the most commonly asked questions and their solutions. Click a question to view detailed answers.

How do I back up my chat history?

To back up your chats:
1. Open WhatsApp > tap More options at the bottom right
2. Go to Settings > Chats > Chat backup
3. Tap "Back Up Now" to start backup to Google Drive or iCloud
You can also set up automatic backup frequency: daily, weekly, or monthly.

How do I enable two-step verification?

Steps to enable two-step verification:
1. Open WhatsApp > tap More options at the bottom right
2. Go to Settings > Account > Two-step verification
3. Tap "Enable"
4. Set a 6-digit PIN and confirm
5. Provide a backup email (optional)
We recommend enabling two-step verification to enhance account security.

How do I create a group?

To create a group:
1. Open WhatsApp > tap the New icon at the top right
2. Select "New Group"
3. Select at least one contact, then tap Next
4. Enter a group name (up to 25 characters)
5. Tap Create
A new group can have up to 256 members; you can add or remove members anytime.

How do I block a contact?

Steps to block a contact:
1. Open the chat with the contact you want to block
2. Tap the contact's name at the top
3. Scroll down and select "Block Contact"
4. Confirm the block

Once blocked, the contact will not be able to send you messages, call you, or see your "last seen" status. You can manage your blocked list in Settings > Account > Privacy > Blocked Contacts.
